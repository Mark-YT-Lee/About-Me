About - Mark Li / 李O廷

Mark Li, born in 2002 and currently residing in Kaohsiung, Taiwan, is a member of the new generation engaged in the field of mechanical research. He collaborates and exchanges ideas with professors and experts from various disciplines, dedicating himself to advancing research and innovation in mechanical engineering.

He enjoys taking unconventional approaches to stand out and leave a memorable impression. He believes that the human brain is the most powerful computer—a priceless gift from his mother, freely given at birth.
