The purpose of this website

This website showcases Mark Li’s academic journey and professional expertise in mechanical engineering, emphasizing his collaborative research and innovative projects across various disciplines.

Through the presentation of his design work, research achievements, and hands-on project experiences, the site highlights his strengths in mechanical design and fosters dialogue between industry and academia to drive ongoing innovation.

It also serves as a personal experiment in web design—a spontaneous idea that turned into a rewarding learning experience. Now that it's built, hopefully it won't go unseen.
